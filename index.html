Retirement Calculator

Will you run out of money?

gwmho
programmer

Problem

Many people do not know when they will run out of money after they have retired. The reason is that they do not have good projections of their financial statuses. Some people have to cope with poverty, get into debt, or return back to work after retirement.

Solution

The solution is a retirement calculator. It allows a user to see his financial future. After a user enters a few pieces of personal information, the application will project his investment value, income and spending each year.
